# World and Environment

## Movement System
- Top-down perspective
- Final Fantasy-style movement with diagonal options
- Each cardinal diagonal counts as 1 tile/roll
- Movement affects combat engagement chances

## Environment Interaction
### Area Detection
- Heartbeat monitor system for area checks
- Dynamic enemy placement based on perception
- Environmental hazards and traps

### Stealth System
- Perception-based detection
- Movement speed affects stealth chances
- Enemy awareness states

## Dungeons and Areas
### Boss Encounters
- Special Soulslike gates for boss areas (Needs a key or similar item to progress)
- Unique combat mechanics for bosses
- Level rewards upon completion

### Random Encounters
- Distance detection affected by perception
- Stealth and awareness mechanics
- Opportunity for preemptive actions

## Survival Elements
### Environment Effects
- Various terrain types (Caves, Mines etc.)
- Weather conditions (if applicable)
- Safe zones after dungeon (inns, towns)

### Resource Management
- Finding food and water
- Quality of resources
- Risk/reward of consuming questionable items
