# Items and Equipment

## Weapons
### Random Generation
- Weapon stats scale with dungeon level
- Rarity system affects sell value and stats/perks
- Special weapon properties can be found

### Weapon Types
#### Ranged Weapons
- Different tiers of weapons (e.g. Tier I pistol)
- Energy weapons with charge mechanics
- Multiple ammunition types

#### Melee Weapons
- Power-based attack system
- Various weapon classes
- Special attack properties

## Equipment Properties
### Rarity System
- Affects item value
- Influences special properties
- Tied to dungeon level progression

### Special Properties
- Can include combat perks
- May have unique abilities
- Some items may have drawbacks or negative effects

## Inventory Management
### Carry Weight
- Affected by Strength and Endurance
- Limits what can be carried
- Strategic inventory decisions

### Item Usage
- Combat items
- Healing items
- Survival items (food, water)
- Can use potentially dangerous items (rancid food, contaminated water)
